Drink of the Moment:
BUTTERFLY PEA LATTE

What is a BUTTERFLY PEA LATTE?

Calling all lovers of beautiful and unique drinks! Look no further than the butterfly pea latte, a stunning beverage that's as captivating to the eye as it is delightful to the taste. This caffeine-free (or caffeine-optional) latte boasts a vibrant blue hue thanks to its star ingredient: butterfly pea flowers. These pretty petals not only add a mesmerizing color but also possess subtle earthy notes that complement the creamy milk and your choice of sweetener. The flowers themselves don't have much flavor, but they steep into a vibrant blue liquid that forms the base of the latte. Here's a quick rundown:

  • Key Ingredient: Butterfly pea flowers (either loose or ground into a powder)
  • Taste: Mildly floral or earthy, but mostly the flavor comes from the added ingredients like milk, sweetener, and spices.
  • Color: Striking blue! The acidity of what you mix it with can change the color a bit lemon juice can turn it purple!
  • Caffeine: None, making it a great alternative to coffee or black tea lattes.

This week's drink of the week is a beautiful and caffeine-free latte made with butterfly pea flowers. The flowers themselves don't have much flavor, but they steep into a vibrant blue liquid that forms the base of the latte. We add a touch of honey and vanilla to give it a sweet and floral flavor.

The butterfly pea latte is a great choice for those who are looking for a healthy and refreshing alternative to coffee or black tea lattes. It's also perfect for those who are looking for a unique and Instagram-worthy drink.

Come try our BUTTERFLY PEA LATTE today!
