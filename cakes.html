Catefe's Classic TIRAMISU:
A Taste of Italy in Every Bite

How is a great classic Tiramisu created at Catefe?

Catefe is proud to present our classic tiramisu, made with an authentic Italian recipe. Our tiramisu is a perfect combination of ladyfingers soaked in espresso, rich mascarpone cream, and a dusting of cocoa powder. Each bite offers a delicious blend of sweet, savory, and slightly bitter flavors that will leave you wanting more.

The process of making tiramisu at Catefe:

  • Ladyfingers: The cookies are baked with simple ingredients like eggs, sugar, and flour, resulting in a perfect soft and airy texture.
  • Espresso: The coffee is carefully brewed to achieve a strong and refined flavor.
  • Mascarpone cream: The cream is made with mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, and a touch of Marsala wine, creating a rich and delicious taste.
  • Assembly: The ladyfingers are dipped in espresso and layered, then topped with mascarpone cream and cocoa powder. The cake is chilled to allow the flavors to blend perfectly.

At Catefe, we take pride in using a traditional recipe that has been passed down through generations. This time-honored method preserves the authentic taste of Italy, delivering a tiramisu that is both familiar and exciting. We are passionate about creating tiramisu that is not only delicious but also a work of art. We invite you to taste the difference and experience the true essence of this classic Italian dessert.
